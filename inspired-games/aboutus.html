Information about our games

Elaborate Tales is our upcoming game. The game offers a unique approach to talking to characters in video games. You can go into the character's mind and alter their memories or just live their experiences. You never know what goes on in people's minds. Sometimes it's peaceful, sometimes it's a monster chasing you endlessly. Can you survive the Elaborate Tales from the people of Cailm Land?

Coordination Correction is a unique take on the puzzle solving experience. This 8-bit like game has you on your tippy toes when trying to solve complex puzzles and take back the crimson gem you rightfully deserve. Which was taken from you at the start of the game. While either being chased down, about to be eaten by monsters, or having a small amount of time to solve a puzzle efore you get sucked in by a black hole; Can you solve these puzzles before these horrible things happen to you?

Who we are

We are a small indie game studio company that is passionate about making unique, high pace action video games. Since we are small, we only have two games available for download right now (check out the download page for more information) and one game, Elaborate Tales, coming out soon.
